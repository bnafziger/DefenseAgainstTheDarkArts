This assignment is worth 24 points. You must complete this assignment individiually, on your own.

Objectives

  • Learn how to read and dissect files and sensitive information (including usernames and passwords) from packet sets using open source tools
  • Work with real network traffic from arguably the world's most dangerous computer network
  • Understand the dangers of sending data over an open network unencrypted, "in-the-clear"
  • Learn and utilize encoding schemes

Overview

You are given four sets of packet captures from two different networks in PCAP format to analyze: two from a network where file transfers occurred, and the other from arguably the world's most hostile network, DEF CON in Las Vegas, NV.

Note: I am not responsible for the contents in the PCAPs from DEF CON.

set2.pcap

However, the data looks weird for a single picture, unlike anything you have seen before. Reconstruct the picture that was sent covertly. HINT: take a look at the last packet and notice the payload data.

10. How many packets are there in this set?

12. Briefly describe how you were able to reconstruct the image from the packets.

13. Who is in the picture?
